Nigeria

Nigeria is a West African country and the most poppulous nation in Africa, with over 200 million people. It is known for its diverse cultures, languages, and ethnic groups, including the Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo. The country has a rich history, from ancient kingdoms like Benin and Oyo to British colonial rule and independence in 1960. Nigeria is Africa's largest economy, driven by oil, agriculture and a growing tech industry. Its capital is Abuja, while Lagos is its Largest city and economic hub. Despite challenges like corruption and insecurity, Nigeria remains influential in African politics, culture and business.
